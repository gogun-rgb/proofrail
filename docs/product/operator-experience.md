# Operator Experience

## Authority

This document is authoritative for human operator experience direction.

## Operator Role

Humans are primarily:

- operational readers
- policy owners
- exception handlers
- governance participants

Human operators need fast understanding of what happened, what is missing, and what requires attention.

## Future Interface Priorities

The future operator surface should prioritize:

- Verdict
- stable reason codes
- human explanation
- missing Evidence
- contradictory Evidence
- Evidence Lineage
- operator attention required
- policy status
- integration status

## Design Prohibitions

The future operator surface MUST NOT use:

- gradient-heavy AI branding
- glowing borders
- decorative AI orbs
- chat-first primary interaction
- sparkle iconography as product language
- confidence percentages
- excessive dashboard cards
- charts without an operational question
- vague copy such as "AI is analyzing your code"

## Current Phase

No operator UI is implemented in Phase 0.
